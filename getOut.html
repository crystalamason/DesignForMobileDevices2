Get Out

Released on: 17/03/17

Genre: Comedy, Horror

Running Time: 104 minutes



‘Get Out’ is the bone-chilling story of Chris’ first meeting with his girlfriend’s parents, who didn’t know he’s black. And lots of black men have gone missing in their neighbourhood…


At first they seem friendly, if a little nervous, but a strange, sinister undercurrent runs through every conversation. And when Chris learns the terrible truth, he needs to find a way to escape – but it’s easier said than done.


The directorial debut of Jordan Peele, ‘Get Out’ is a tense, twisty thriller about the horror of racism in white suburbia.
