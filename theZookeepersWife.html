The Zookeeper's Wife

Released on: 21/04/17

Genre: Drama

Running Time: 127 minutes



Antonina Zabinska was ‘The Zookeeper’s Wife’ in Warsaw, 1939. She and her husband worked tirelessly at the zoo, until their lives took a dark turn when Nazi Germany invaded Poland.


As the Nazi troops began their reign of terror, Warsaw’s Jews were rounded up and forced to live in the Ghetto. Desperate to make a difference, Antonina began working with the resistance, and helped save the lives of hundreds of Jews by hiding them in her zoo.


Experience the incredible true story of ‘The Zookeeper’s Wife’, starring Jessica Chastain and Daniel Bruhl.
