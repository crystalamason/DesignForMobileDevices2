The Boss Baby

Released on: 07/04/17

Genre: Animated, Comedy, Family

Running Time: 97 minutes



When Tim Templeton found out he was getting a baby brother, he couldn't possibly have prepared himself for the arrival of ‘The Boss Baby', a suit-wearing, briefcase-carrying, hyper-intelligent little boy.


Soon, Tim discovers the Boss Baby has a secret mission: there's a finite amount of love in the world, and a dastardly plot is afoot to steal it from babies. Tim and his brother need to find out who's behind it and put a stop to their schemes.


Alec Baldwin brings his voice and business acumen to the role of ‘The Boss Baby', a hilarious animated comedy from the director of ‘Madagascar'.
